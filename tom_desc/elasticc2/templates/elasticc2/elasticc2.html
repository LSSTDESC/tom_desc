The ELAsTiCC2 Campaign

A 3 years of LSST DIA alerts, a follow on to the first ELAsTiCC campaign. The simulated alerts were sent in Nov-Dec 2023. Four brokers ingested those alerts and provided classifications back here. The ELAsTiCC2 data set includes:

  • 4,119,322 diaObject (individual transients or variables)
  • 62,472,882 [CHECK THIS] diaSource (detections, for which an alert was sent out)
  • 987,156,194 [CHECK THIS] diaForcedSource (measurements; includes everything in diaSource)
  • 377,345,184 classification messages from 20 different broker classifiers (most brokers had more than one classifier).

For general information, see The DESC ELAsTiCC Challenge.

For information about querying the results of the ELAsTiCC2 dataset and calculating metrics, see https://github.com/LSSTDESC/elasticc_metrics

Streaming and Database Statistics:

  • Graphs of hourly streaming rate of original ELAsTiCC alerts sent to the ZADS broker (updated daily)
  • Broker Time Delays — histogram of time delays between alerts being isused and ingesting broker classifications (currently updated manually)
  • Broker Completeness Vs. Time — fraction of alerts sent for which we have received broker classifications, divided by classifier and time interval.
